# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist



1. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone.
2. Do not approve unknown "collect requests" on UPI apps.
3. Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies.
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media.
5. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.
6. Install apps only from trusted sources like the Google Play Store or App Store.
7. Do not install screen-sharing apps when instructed by unknown persons.
8. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history for any suspicious activity.



## UPI / Financial Safety Tips

1. Always double-check the receiver’s name before sending money.
2. Remember: You do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
